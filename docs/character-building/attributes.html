Attributes

Each character has attributes, think of them as natural proclivities for certain physical abilities. Some people just naturally good at something and character attributes reflect that. You cannot straight up level up your attributes as your character gets more points (Though they can be modified by gained traits).

Baseline level is 8, attributes of this level reflects average human performance in given type of activity.

For player characters recommended starting Attribute Points are 72. So each character gets 8 points to spend on attributes.
