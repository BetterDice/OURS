Skills

Skills. They determine how good you character is in certain type of activity, these are acquired competences and can be leveled up during the game. In most campaigns skills are generalized, unless you want to go deep in certain category. For example in medieval combat centered campaign you might want to spilt Melee to Melee (Swords), Melee (Polearms), Melee (Clubs) etc.

Level 8 of any skill represent average familiarity (which grants only a 50% chance to fail succeed).

80 Skill points is basic amount of point each character gets. At the creation of character max level of skill can not exceed 15.

Melee ⚔️​

Expertise in combat using melee weapons such as swords, clubs, and axes. This skill encompasses techniques for both offensive and defensive maneuvers, understanding weapon reach, and exploiting weaknesses in an opponent's defense. Masters of melee combat are often trained in various weapon types and fighting styles.

Brawl 👊​

Skill in hand-to-hand combat, including martial arts, boxing, and wrestling. This involves striking, grappling, and using the environment to one's advantage. Brawlers are adept at close-quarters combat, leveraging their physical strength and agility to subdue opponents without the use of weapons.

Ranged 🏹​

Proficiency with all ranged weaponry available at the current tech level, including bows, firearms, energy weapons and all kinds of artillery. This skill covers aiming, shooting, and maintaining ranged weapons, as well as understanding projectile trajectories and environmental factors that affect accuracy. Skilled marksmen can hit targets at varying distances with precision.

Tactics 🌮​

Ability to devise and execute strategic plans, find advantageous positions, and outsmart enemies in small group combat. This skill covers understanding battlefield dynamics, coordinating team movements, and exploiting enemy weaknesses. Tactical thinkers can turn the tide of a skirmish with clever strategies and effective leadership.

Crafting 🛠️​

Skill in creating, repairing, and maintaining various items, including weapons, tools, and machinery. This includes understanding materials, construction techniques, and the use of specialized tools. Craftsmen can produce custom items and perform intricate repairs that keep equipment in optimal condition.

Technology 💾​

Knowledge and proficiency in using and understanding the technical aspects of the world, including computers, electronics, explosives and advanced machinery. This skill covers programming, hacking, and operating complex systems. Technologically adept individuals can manipulate digital environments and innovate new technological solutions.

Medicine ⚕️​

Ability to provide first aid, perform surgery, and possess general medical knowledge for treating injuries and illnesses. This skill covers diagnosing conditions, administering treatments, and understanding medical procedures. Medical professionals can save lives and maintain the health of a group in various situations.

Survival ⛺​

Ability to survive in the wilderness, including skills in foraging, taming animals, navigating, and building shelter. This skill encompasses knowledge of edible plants, tracking, and weather prediction. Survivalists can thrive in harsh environments and provide for themselves and others using natural resources.

Science 🔬​

Understanding of academic science and general knowledge in fields such as biology, chemistry, physics, and more. This skill allows individuals to conduct experiments, analyze data, and apply scientific principles to solve problems. Scientists can innovate new technologies and understand the natural world at a fundamental level.

Athletics 👟​

Physical prowess and capability in activities requiring strength, endurance, agility, and coordination. This skill includes running, jumping, swimming, and climbing. Athletes can perform feats of physical endurance and dexterity, making them invaluable in situations requiring quick movement and physical challenges.

Driving 🚘​

Ability to operate all kinds of vehicles, from cars and motorcycles to boats and aircraft. This skill covers vehicle control, respective navigation, and maintenance. Skilled drivers can perform high-speed maneuvers, evade pursuers, and handle various terrains and weather conditions.

Stealth 🥷​

Skill in hiding, concealing objects or actions, and performing tasks without being detected. This includes moving silently, using cover, and avoiding surveillance. Stealthy individuals can infiltrate secure areas, gather intelligence, and execute tasks without drawing attention.

Talking 🗣️​

Ability to effectively communicate, lie, persuade, detect lies, and threaten others. This skill includes understanding body language, tone, and context to influence and read people. Charismatic individuals can sway crowds, negotiate deals, and uncover hidden motives through conversation.

Social 🗿​

Knowledge of social norms, etiquette, and customs, as well as understanding social dynamics and interactions. This skill allows individuals to navigate complex social situations, build and maintain relationships, and understand the subtleties of social hierarchies and traditions.

Lore 📚​

Knowledge of religious, mystical, and occult practices, beliefs, and lore. This skill involves understanding rituals, interpreting omens, and interacting with supernatural forces. Practitioners of occultism can perform magical rituals, summon entities, and gain insight into hidden mysteries.

Arts 🎨​

Knowledge and skill in performing and creating in any chosen artistic field, such as music, painting, writing, and acting. This skill includes creative expression, technical proficiency, and an understanding of artistic traditions. Artists can evoke emotions, tell stories, and create works that inspire and entertain.
